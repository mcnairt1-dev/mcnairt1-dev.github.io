Overall Impact of Technology on the Arts</a></td></tr>


<tr><td><strong>Author</strong></td><td>Pew Research Center</td></tr>


<tr><td><strong>Abstract</strong></td>

<td>

Technology has increased access to art by allowing artists to share their work online with global audiences. Social media and digital platforms help artists promote themselves without relying on traditional galleries. This shift makes the art world more inclusive and gives emerging artists greater visibility. Online engagement also allows audiences to interact directly with artists, creating stronger connections and broader cultural impact.
