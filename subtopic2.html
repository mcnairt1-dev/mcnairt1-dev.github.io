<tr><td><strong>Abstract</strong></td>

<td>

Artificial intelligence has become an important part of contemporary art by allowing artists to create works using algorithms and generative systems. Artists can input data into programs that produce new images and patterns, challenging traditional ideas of authorship. AI enables artists to explore complex visuals that would be difficult to create manually. This technology pushes the boundaries of creativity and raises questions about the role of machines in artistic expression.
